Motivational for Wellness

Your best gift to yourself and your child to be born

By Ashok Vankineni on Thursday, July 26, 2018

Case: one fully pregnant lady was not getting labour pains even after 38 weeks of pregnancy. The doctor gave her medicine for labour pains. When the pains started, she could not bear them and hence she was given anaesthesia. Because she is in sedation she was not pushing the baby out. The doctors waited for one day. Meanwhile, her water broke down, but the baby did not come out. Then they did caesarean. By then baby got a clot in brain, resulting one side limbs impaired.

Now the baby is growing. Chances are there for the effect of clot also to increase. They have started physio-therapy for the baby. All this could be avoided, had the mother done some yoga, during the pregnancy. What they spend now is much more higher than what it would have costed for prenatal yoga, money as well as time. Baby would have had perfect health, physically as well as mentally, which is invaluable.

Perfect body, health and powerful mind – these are the best gifts you can give to your children by spending some time doing yoga. You gift yourself with joyful pregnancy period, easy & and painless delivery.

Pressures we experience, the diseases we suffer are definitely reflected in the genes of embryo. Yoga for 6 months to one year prior to conception, will make great difference in child’s health & psychology.

And continuing prenatal yoga, after conception will make

  1. a) The carrying period much more pleasant, reduced fear of labour pains
  2. b) More healthy, less complications
  3. c) Reduce labour pains, almost nill musculo- skeletal pains
  4. d) Short time labour and easy delivery
  5. e) Healthy growth of baby and healthy mother
  6. f) Strong bondage between baby and mother
  7. g) Active and intelligent baby
  8. h) Less postnatal stress to mother
  9. i) Quick recovery and toning of muscles and so on

Two people get injure by accidents, out of 100 insured. Still we don’t mind  paying for accidental risk courage, every year. 36% miscarriages are happening due to various reasons.  10% babies are born with various health issues.

Yoga is much more beyond the insurance. Yoga is your responsibility towards health and happiness of your child.
