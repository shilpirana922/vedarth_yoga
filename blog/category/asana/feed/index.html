]]> Natarajasana :-

English name:

  • Lord of the Dance Pose

Asana/pose Category: 

  • Balance
  • Backbend
  • Standing
  • Chest opener

Benefits:-

  • Strengthens Ankle & Legs
  • Stretches Thorax,  Abdomen, Thigh, Inguinal region, Shoulder

Preparatory Asanas (Poses)

  • Vriksasana
  • Dhanurasana
  • Ustrasnaa

Contra-indications:-

  • Avoid if you suffer from low blood pressure.

Note:
1. Consult a doctor before beginning yogaasanas
2. Learn Yoga always under Certified yoga teacher

]]> Chaturanga Dandasana: 

English name:

  • Four-limbed staff pose.
  • Also known as Low Plank

Asana/pose Category: 

  • Arm Balance
  • Core

Benefits: 

  • Strengthens Arms
  • Strengthens Wrists
  • Strengthens Core

Preparatory Asanas (Poses)

  • Phalakasana
  • Urdhvamukha shvanasana

Contra-indications:

Avoid this asana (pose) if you have

  • Shoulder pain
  • Cervical Spondylitis
  • Wrist pain

Note:
1. Consult a doctor before beginning yogaasanas
2. Learn Yoga always under Certified yoga teacher
