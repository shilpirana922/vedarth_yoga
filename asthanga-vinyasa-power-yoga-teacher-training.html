Asthanga Vinyasa/Power Yoga Teacher Training

Welcome to Yoga

Objective:

Bodhi yoga offers Ashtanga Vinyasa /Power Yoga Teacher Training Courses for practitioners of all levels who are looking to explore and deepen their practices, learn the art of teaching, and experience the ancient science of Ashtanga Yoga. Our training course will prepare you with all the tools and confidence you need to become a yoga teacher and will propel you forward on your personal yoga journey. The Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga Teacher Training Course will enliven your body and enlighten your mind and spirit with the knowledge and experience of yoga.

Journey with Bodhi Ashtanga Vinyasa /Power:

Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga is a modern form of yoga that is in trend nowadays. The name, ‘Ashtanga’ was derived after the eight limbs of yoga mentioned in sutras of yoga Pathanjali. But why should one opt for Ashtanga yoga teacher training in India? Ashtanga yoga mainly emphasizes on improving flexibility, breathing, and relaxation of the body. The aim of this type of yoga is solely the improvement in overall health of the person practicing it.

Course:

The course presents strong emphasis on the Traditional Ashtanga Vinyasa Primary series. Trainees expected to maintain a daily Yoga practice during the training.

This course will provide:

  • Asana Primary series
  • Yogic breathing practice
  • Anatomy and Physiology for yoga
  • Philosophy of Pathanjali Yoga Sutras
  • Teaching methodology
  • Active & Passive Meditations
  • Ethics and business of Yoga.

What You Gain

Trainees will be able to practice and teach primary series of Ashtanga asana sequence, How to give hands on adjustment.

  • Trainees will learn Yogic breathing techniques called Pranayama
  • Trainees will learn Philosophy of Pathanjali Yoga sutras Samadhi Pada & Sadhana Pada.
  • Trainees will learn Active and passive meditation techniques.
  • Trainees will learn Applied anatomy for yoga and understand articulation of body parts based on asanas









SKILL LEVEL:

Prior to attending a yoga teacher training, we recommend that students must have been practicing yoga regularly for a minimum of three months, or have a closely related sports training or fitness background.

Prior notice is required in case of Practitioners having major injuries, conical health issues or psychological issues

Note: Most importantly, we ask that students come to our trainings with an open mind and an open heart, ready to embrace the learning experience, and to be supportive of themselves and their fellow students with strong desire.

Benefits

  • This Course will make you a perfect professional who is able to teach Ashtanga yoga to a layman. Each movement or posture practiced in Ashtanga yoga has its own importance that you need to teach well.
  • By the end of the course, you will be able to confidently teach a modified Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga class suitable for all levels and offer modifications for students with injuries or physical limitations.
